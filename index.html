Plant Knower AI App

Youssef Moaz - Grade 6

📱💻 Project Overview

'Plant Knower AI' is a smart app for phones and computers.

It helps you find out the names of plants and talk to an AI about them.

The idea is to make learning about plants easy and fun using AI!

🧭 What Can The App Do?

When you open the app or website, you can choose:

  • 💬 Chatbot: Talk to the AI
  • 📸 Plant Identifier: Upload a plant picture

💬 Feature: Chatbot

  • Chat directly with an AI about plants.
  • You can even send a picture in the chat.
  • The AI tells you about the plant in the picture.
  • Ask more questions about how to care for it, what it's good for, or anything!

📸 Feature: Plant Identifier

  • Upload a picture of a plant.
  • The app figures out what plant it is using special technology.
  • It shows you details like:
    • Plant Type
    • Best Place to Grow
    • How to Take Care of It
    • What It's Good For
    • ...and more!

🎯 Why Is This Project Important?

  • Helps people who love plants learn their names easily.
  • Uses cool AI technology to help people and nature.
  • It's a fun way to learn about the plants around us!

🛠️ Tools I Used

Making it Look Good (Frontend):
  • HTML, CSS, JavaScript
The Smart Parts (AI):
  • Chatbot AI
  • Plant Identifier AI
  • An AI helper ([bolt.new]) for coding
Making the App:
  • Android App (Made with App Creator)
  • Works on Phones & Computers
